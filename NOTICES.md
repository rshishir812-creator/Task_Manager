## Emoji

ChoreQuest renders Unicode emoji characters (🎮, 🛡️, 🔥, ⚔️, 🏆, etc.).
These are Unicode standard codepoints and are free to use. Visual
rendering is provided by the user's operating system emoji font (Apple
Color Emoji on iOS / macOS, Segoe UI Emoji on Windows, Noto Color Emoji
on Android / Linux). ChoreQuest does not ship or redistribute any
emoji font.

## User content

Profile photos are sourced exclusively from Google OAuth
(`lh3.googleusercontent.com`) and remain owned by the user. ChoreQuest
displays them but does not store, modify, or redistribute them.
